Our Quality
statement.

is the result of the sum of individual perspectives in pursuit of a common superior benefit. We believe that through communication; people, companies and organizations can and must grow. Our aim is to contribute to this constant evolution, to help our clients and our teams grow.

Our mission is to generate a positive change in people, companies and organizations through the creation of forward-thinking brands and innovative digital products that focus on obtaining strategic results. Results that we obtain by working with extraordinary people, open, flexible and committed to empowering both our clients and ourselves.

We believe these objectives can be achieved by a constant search for excellence at each point of contact that we have both internally and externally. We base this constant search for quality in the following foundations:

HUMAN VALUE:

  • We believe in the value of people above all things
  • We constantly train in new technologies and tools that help us to better fulfill our commitments
  • We put our client's audience first, that user, that consumer, that organization, that person that ultimately we must listen and communicate effectively to, in order to achieve our clients' objectives.
  • We work with professionalism, commitment and passion.
  • We help our clients understand the importance of each of the tasks.
  • We empathize with our customers. We understand what they are thinking.
  • We lead by example. We hear all voices. We build trust.

INNOVATION:

  • We set aside the necessary time and energy to analyze each new tool available.
  • Week after week we review each new relevant launch in the industry, both branding and digital.
  • We seek to always be updated on where the world is heading to.
  • We want to be part of that change, and we contribute to it with our knowledge.
  • We respect each other's perspectives and ideas.

OBTAINING RESULTS:

  • We know that those who choose us seek to enhance their business, therefore, our decisions are 100% strategic.
  • The key to success is never losing your curiosity. We always go further.
  • We believe in the value of measurement of results, sales, impact and culture.
  • Our approach allows us to obtain the expected results.
  • We prioritize success in everything we do.
  • We always do our best.

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT:

  • We learn from our mistakes through regular reviews and feedback at the end of each project.
  • Our processes and work tools are continually updated in order to always be working in the most effective and efficient way.
  • Our goal is to exploit the collective genius of all parties involved.

CLEAR AGREEMENTS:

  • We believe in agreements and we respect personal relationships so much that we put all those “difficult” points in them so that later the process is as easy as possible.
  • We are transparent about what happens both internally and externally.
  • Although we agree on different number of reviews, we understand that sometimes it is necessary to make efforts from both parties to reach the desired result. And in those cases, we always prioritize the satisfaction of our customers.
  • Our absolute premise is to respect the agreements, both at the scope level and at the time level. And in case any of the two points can’t meet what was agreed, we always have a commitment to give a little more.

With this declaration of "Quality Policies" we simply want to tell the world how we think, how we act and, above all, how we communicate. Our vision is to inspire the world and be a benchmark in our industry, and we believe that it is only possible by leading by example.

We design for brands, we work for people.

Wisnu Strw

CEO / Founder
